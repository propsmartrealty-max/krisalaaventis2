What does 2.25 BHK and 3.25 BHK mean?

The "+0.25" refers to an additional dedicated cubicle space designed for flexibility — usable as a home office, children's study corner, or a compact reading nook. It's Krisala's BigHeartLiving philosophy in action.

What is Aluform Technology?

Aluform uses aluminium formwork for the entire RCC (Reinforced Concrete Construction) structure. It produces superior surface finish, greater structural accuracy, is earthquake-resistant, and reduces construction timelines significantly compared to conventional brick-block methods.

How far is Krisala Aventis from Hinjawadi IT Park?
