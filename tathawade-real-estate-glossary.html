Key Market Terms

MahaRERA

The Real Estate Regulatory Authority of Maharashtra. It ensures transparency and accountability in the sector. Krisala Aventis is fully RERA-compliant: P52100080336.

Carpet Area

The actual usable area within the walls of an apartment. It does not include the thickness of inner walls or common areas. Krisala Aventis offers optimized carpet areas in 2.25 & 3.25 BHK types.

Aluform Technology

A modern construction system using aluminium formwork. It provides superior strength, mirror-finish walls, and faster construction speed compared to traditional brickwork.

Floor Area Ratio (FAR)

The ratio of a building's total floor area to the size of the piece of land upon which it is built. In PCMC, FAR regulations govern the density and height of residential towers.
